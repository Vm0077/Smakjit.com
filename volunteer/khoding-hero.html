Khoding Hero 2022

CADT

01 December 2022

1 . What is Khoding Hero?

The Khoding Hero program was designed to give university students an opportunity to participate in local development through STEM outreach in rural areas where there is a lack of Digital and ICT skills information, particularly at the university level,” said Chanda Pen, chief of party for the USAID Digital Workforce


2 . Overview

KHoding Hero is a program that goes through a comprehensive process to recruit volunteers consisting of talented students that are excellent in academics and passionate to share their knowledge with the younger generation Through the recruitment process. The program will be conducted in two districts, Romeas Haek District and Romdoul District, Svay Rieng Province. The volunteers will be performed as a team (4 members each) and play a mentor role for the students while living with the locals there. H.E Mr. HEAN Samboeun shared that “ This program aims to encourage young students in rural areas to learn more and pursue digital technology skills. We know that this initiative will contribute to the future development of Cambodia. CADT wishes that all the volunteers will be completed this program successfully and also became an inspiration to the younger generation.".
